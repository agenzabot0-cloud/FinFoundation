# COMMUNITY GUIDELINES

Be respectful to all players. Harassment, hate speech, threats, targeted abuse, or discriminatory language will not be tolerated. Keep chat family‑friendly and avoid profanity during online play.

Protect privacy and safety. Do not share or post anyone’s private information or personal data (including real names, addresses, phone numbers, or other identifying details). Doxxing or encouraging others to reveal private information is prohibited.

No cheating or exploits. Using hacks, cheats, exploits, or unauthorized third‑party tools that give an unfair advantage or destabilize the game is strictly forbidden. If you discover an exploit, report it to the moderators instead of abusing it.

License and server rules. Do not operate, sell, or advertise servers or services that violate the game’s free-client license or harm other players. Commercial services that circumvent licensing terms or negatively impact the community are not allowed.

No scams or spam. Scamming, phishing, spam, pyramid schemes, and unsolicited solicitations are banned. Do not use the game or its channels to advertise or recruit for schemes that exploit other players.

Follow in‑game rules and moderator instructions. Respect any additional server- or channel-specific rules and comply with directions from moderators. If a rule is unclear, ask a moderator for clarification before acting.

Reporting and non-retaliation. If you see a violation, report it to the moderation team using the in‑game report feature, by opening an issue, or by DMing a moderator. Do not take matters into your own hands or retaliate against other players for reporting or being reported.

Enforcement and consequences. Moderators may take actions including warnings, temporary mutes, temporary bans, or permanent bans depending on the severity and frequency of violations. Serious or repeated offenses will result in stronger penalties.

Appeals. If you believe a moderation action was unjust, you may submit an appeal within seven days. Include a clear explanation and any relevant context; moderators will review appeals and respond as promptly as possible.
